Nutz for PC

Rising from the flames comes Eternal Greatness

Nutz for PC was founded originally as a private pest control friend's chat, operating on worlds 53 and 69. Over the years as pest control and the game has evolved we have moved away from only doing pest control and take part in a myriad of other activities. Nutz for PC is home to over a hundred players and are the envy of other clans with our Tier 7 clan citadel. Our home world is world 69 but you can find many players traversing across a multitude of worlds engaging in activities including skilling, bossing, minigames and more. With rich history comes a wealth of knowledgable players. Members in Nutz for PC are not only friendly but are willing to engage with members old and new to take advantage of all that runescape has to offer. With streamers and a dedicated teamspeak you are sure to be able to find what you are looking for in a clan by joining Nutz for PC, if hesitent just join our clan as a guest and feel free to ask clanmates or staff questions.
