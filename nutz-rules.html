Nutz for PC Clan rules

So you want to join Nutz for PC, before you embark on the journey that may change your Runescape career please read through following rules which you are required to follow as a member of our clan

  • Obey all Runescape rules. Not only are you required to obey clan rules but all rules put forth by Jagex should too be followed
  • Be respectful of other clan member. You wouldn't want people disrespecting you so treat others the way you want to be treated. If you have issues with someone please just add them to your ignore list.
  • No cursing. Since Runescape is played by many a range of players we do not want anybody being offended, that being said a curse free environment also encourages less hostility.
  • Respect staff. Not only should you respect clanmates, try to make sure to listen to staff if they tell you something, expecially if your actions may be causing another player issues or might be breaking rules.
  • No taking advantage of clanmates. Scamming, luring or taking advantage of another clanmate is prohibited and will result in an immediate temporary suspension from the clan.
  • Cap weekly. Take advantage of the bountiful experience to be gained in our tier 7 citadel and try to cap whenever you can.
  • Have fun. Make sure you have fun, take part in clan events and join in with discussions to make your experience and others' as enjoyable as possible.
